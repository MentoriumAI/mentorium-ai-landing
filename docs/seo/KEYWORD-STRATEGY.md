## üèÜ Competitor Landscape (Peru)

### Main Competitors:

**1. Google Classroom**
- **Market Position:** Dominant, especially post-pandemic
- **Strengths:** Free, easy, Google integration
- **Weaknesses:** No administrative functions, no SIAGIE integration, no payment management
- **Our Strategy:** Position as "more than an aula virtual" with complete administrative solution

**2. Moodle**
- **Market Position:** Most widespread globally, used by PUCP (Paideia), UCV (Clementina)
- **Strengths:** Free, open-source, flexible
- **Weaknesses:** Complex setup, requires technical knowledge, poor UX, no modern AI features
- **Our Strategy:** Emphasize modern UX, AI capabilities, and integrated support

**3. Canvas LMS**
- **Market Position:** Strong in universities (PUCP, USMP, UTP, USIL)
- **Strengths:** Robust features, free basic version
- **Weaknesses:** Limited in K-12, no school-specific admin modules
- **Our Strategy:** Target K-12 market with school-specific features

**4. Blackboard**
- **Market Position:** High-end, mainly universities (U. Lima, Pac√≠fico, UPC)
- **Strengths:** Enterprise features, robust functionality
- **Weaknesses:** Expensive, complex, overkill for K-12
- **Our Strategy:** Position as affordable, agile alternative for schools

**5. BlinkLearning**
- **Market Position:** Growing in private schools, 4,500+ schools globally
- **Strengths:** Editorial content integration
- **Weaknesses:** Focuses on content, not comprehensive management
- **Our Strategy:** Offer integrated content + management + AI
